Types of Lenses for Cataract Detection

Monofocal Lens

Monofocal lenses are the most common type of intraocular lenses used in cataract surgery. They provide clear vision at one distance (near, intermediate, or far).

Price: ₹15,000

Multifocal Lens

Multifocal lenses provide clear vision at multiple distances, reducing the need for glasses after cataract surgery.

Price: ₹30,000

Toric Lens

Toric lenses are designed to correct astigmatism and provide clear vision at a single distance.

Price: ₹25,000

Light Adjustable Lens

The first and only intraocular lens (IOL) that can be altered following cataract surgery. It allows you to adjust and preview your vision until it satisfies your preferences and lifestyle needs.

Price: ₹40,000

Which Lens is Right for You?

Lens Type Best For Advantages Disadvantages
Monofocal Lens Clear vision at one distance Affordable, reliable Requires glasses for other distances
Multifocal Lens Clear vision at multiple distances Reduces dependency on glasses Higher cost, may cause glare
Toric Lens Correcting astigmatism Clear vision for astigmatism patients Higher cost, limited to single distance
Light Adjustable Lens Customizable vision Adjustable after surgery, precise vision Most expensive, requires follow-up visits
